Saturday Market

This is our most popular market year round. During the spring and summer you can expect to visit us at the Comox Valley Fair Grounds. In the Fall, come and find us at the Native Son's Hall parking lot and perhaps grab a beer from Gladstones after! During the winter we move inside so you all can stay cozy and warm.

Wednesday Market

Established in 1997, our mid-week market provides farmers an additional platform to sell crops harvested during the week. Located in Downtown Courtenay, the market collaborates with Comox Valley Arts Council and Downtown Courtenay Business Improvement Association for the Summer Street Market.
