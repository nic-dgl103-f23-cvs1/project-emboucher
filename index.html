Our Markets Join the Community

Join the Community!

There are many ways to engage with our CVFM community beyond just shopping. We love hosting musicians, welcoming new volunteers, and are continuously seeking additional vendors to join our markets.

Our Vendors Volunteer Apply Now!

Volunteer with us

We depend on committed volunteers to run our weekly markets, offering perks like early access, gifts, discounts, and tickets to the Fall Harvest Feast. Join us by volunteering today!

Become a Vendor

Our markets thrive with the support of passionate vendors who contribute to our vibrant community. Enjoy exclusive benefits like prime booth locations, promotional opportunities, and invitations to special events. Elevate your business by becoming a vendor with us today!

CVFM Music

You might visit us to enjoy the music, but have you ever considered playing it? We are always looking for passionate local musicians to promote at our markets and we are so lucky to have such great talent here on Vancouver Island.

Shop our Vendors

If you haven't been to the CVFM yet, let me tell you, you don't even know what you're missing out on yet! Our vendors travel from both far and near (but mostly near) to sell their crafted products to you. With such a variety, how could you not shop local? From lettuce to lemon lattice, we've got you covered.
