Apply to be a Vendor

Elevate your business with ease in 6 simple steps. Join our vibrant community and enjoy exclusive benefits, including prime booth locations and special events.

Pre-Application Verification

  1. Ensure you meet minimum requirements to become a vendor.
  2. Confirm if you are in the Comox Valley or Strathcona Regional District.
  3. Verify that your products fall under eligible categories.

Pre-Application Inquiry

  1. Contact us BEFORE you fill out an application
  2. Email info@cvfm.ca if it's your first application.
    • List of products to sell.
    • Preferred start date.
    • Local ingredients list if applicable.
  3. Wait for approval before proceeding to Step 3.

Documentation Check

  1. Collect and upload:
    • Food Safe/Market Safe Certificate.
    • Confirmation, Health Permit, Facility Inspection, or VIHA docs.
    • Organic Certification (if applicable).
    • CFIA Certification (if applicable).
    • Liquor License or Market Authorization (for Alcohol Vendors).
  2. Food Safe/Market Safe certificate required for all food sales.
  3. Review Guideline for Sale of Foods at Temporary Food Markets for risk levels.
  4. If unsure about a Letter of Confirmation, contact VIHA at 250-331-8518.

Read Rules of Membership

  1. Review the Rules of Membership.
  2. Complete skill testing questions at the end of the application.

Fill out Application Form

  1. Complete online application.
  2. Submit with note if waiting on documentation.
  3. Conditional approval possible; email missing docs to info@cvfm.ca.
  4. Receive automatic confirmation email upon submission.
  5. Monthly review by Application Review Committee.
  6. Expect status email approximately a month after committee meeting.

Membership Dues

  1. Secure your spot in the Market Mix.
  2. New vendors, pay dues within 30 days of approval or first market attendance.
  3. Reapply if dues aren't paid in time (no guaranteed approval).
  4. Fees due upon approval, valid annually from January 1 to December 31.
  5. Membership Dues are NON Refundable.
